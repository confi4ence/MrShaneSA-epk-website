The Artist

Shane Sepheu

Mr Shane SA is a South African deep house DJ and producer based in South Africa, where the quiet depth of the land is woven into every track he creates.

His sound is defined by deep, groove-laden house music warm basslines, lush chords, and melodies that move the dancefloor and linger long after the needle lifts. Whether playing an intimate lounge or a main stage festival slot, his sets carry the same soulful authenticity.

Performing on Pioneer equipment, Mr Shane SA is a proud core member of the 1060 Sounds collective and a rising force in South Africa's underground deep house movement, with collaborations spanning legendary names in the genre.
