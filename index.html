The Artist

Shane Sepheu

Mr Shane SA is a South African deep house DJ and producer rooted in Ga-Masemola, a township in the Sekhukhune region of Limpopo, a place whose quiet depth and soulfulness are woven into every track he creates.

His sound is defined by deep, groove-laden house music that lives in the space between the dancefloor and the soul, warm basslines, lush chords, and melodies that linger long after the needle lifts.

Performing on Pioneer equipment, Mr Shane SA commands dancefloors with the same authenticity that drives his studio work. A proud member of the 1060 Sounds collective, he is a rising force in South Africa's underground deep house movement.
